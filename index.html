Confused, but it's okay

This gets easier, right?

Just finished my second primer class, and I'm leaving the room with even more questions than I came in with. Among the top of my list:

  1. How the fuck do I float things? There's got to be an easier way.
  2. Will I ever use the command line with confidence?
  3. Git. Period.

This is awesome. It's the first time in a while that I've felt so challenged, yet so incredibly able. There's something so satisfying about running into a problem, working through it, and making magic happen. Yes, that's what I call working code; magic. It's unexpected, unexplainable, and always delightful.

I'm looking forward to where this class takes me. My goals are high-- continue on to Full Stack Javascript-- but my spirits are even higher. Pray for me.

Katie Dyer | Monday, 3/9/15
